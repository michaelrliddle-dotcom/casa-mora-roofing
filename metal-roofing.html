Metal Roofing

Sleek standing-seam metal that reflects heat and lasts a lifetime.

Standing seam metal roofing is the modern standard for longevity and performance. Casa Mora installs sleek, weather-tight metal roofs that reflect heat, shed water, and last a lifetime — ideal for both contemporary homes and commercial buildings.

Our metal systems resist fire, wind, and corrosion, and their reflective finish can lower cooling costs through California's hottest months.

KEY BENEFITS

  • Lifespan of 50+ years
  • Reflects heat and lowers energy bills
  • Class A fire resistance
  • Sheds rain and debris effortlessly
  • Sleek, modern standing-seam finish
