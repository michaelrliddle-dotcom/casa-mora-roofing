Attic Ventilation & Cool Roofing

Ventilation and cool-roof systems that lower temps and energy bills.

A great roof is only as good as what's underneath it. Casa Mora designs attic ventilation and cool roofing systems that regulate temperature, reduce moisture, and protect your roof structure from the inside out.

Proper ventilation paired with reflective cool-roof materials keeps your home more comfortable, lowers energy bills, and dramatically extends the life of your roofing system.

KEY BENEFITS

  • Balanced intake & exhaust ventilation
  • Reduces attic heat and moisture buildup
  • Cool-roof materials reflect solar heat
  • Lower energy bills year-round
  • Extends the life of your entire roof system
