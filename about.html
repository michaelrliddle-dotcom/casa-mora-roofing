About Casa Mora

A FAMILY ROOFING COMPANY CALIFORNIA TRUSTS

Casa Mora Roofing is a family-owned and operated roofing company proudly serving homeowners and businesses across Southern and Northern California. For more than 15 years, our family has built its name on a simple promise: do honest work, treat every roof like it's our own, and stand behind it long after the job is done.

We specialize in full roof replacements and repairs for both residential and commercial properties. From the first inspection to the final nail, you work directly with the people who care about the outcome — not a rotating crew of subcontractors. That's the difference a family business makes.

Every project is fully licensed (CSLB Lic # 1155588) and insured, and backed by our 50-year workmanship warranty. Whether you're protecting your home or your investment, you can trust Casa Mora to get it right the first time.
